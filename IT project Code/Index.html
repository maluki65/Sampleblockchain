Blockchain Health Record System

Blockchain's decentralized ledger architecture offers an alternative to the centralize models that dominate traditional healthcare systems. The decentralized nature of blockchain eliminates the need for a central authority, fostering a distributed network where data is stored across multiple nodes. This decentralized approach enhances the security and privacy of patient information by minimizing the risk of a single point of failure or unauthorized access. Furthermore, the immutability feature of blockchain ensures the tamper-resistant nature of records providing an indelible and transparent trail of changes. Once information is recorded on the blockchain, it becomes practically impossible to alter, ensuring the integrity and authenticity of patient medical records. This feature is crucial in a healthcare context where the accuracy of data is paramount for informed decision-making and patient care.
